Pecan Chicken Salad

How to Make Pecan Chicken Salad

his pecan chicken salad made with chopped cooked chicken, herbs, celery, pecans, red onion, and a creamy
homemade dressing is really flavorful. Serve on top of toasted croissants or butter lettuce.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons white wine vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ½ teapoon Park Hill maple pepper
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • ½ cup chopped toasted pecans
  • 2 tablespoons minced red onion

Steps to Follow

  1. Mix mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, vinegar, garlic powder, thyme, and maple pepper together in a
    bowl until well combined. Add chicken, celery, pecans, and red onion; stir well to incorporate.
  2. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 3 days.
